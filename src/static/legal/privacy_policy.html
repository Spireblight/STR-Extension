What Data is Collected

The usage of our extension is logged. The only personally identifiable information stored in the logs is your Twitch login. Apart from that, only information about usage of our extension is stored for debugging purposes and for analyzing traffic. The logs last only couple of weeks and then they are overwritten. They are not being backed up anywhere.
