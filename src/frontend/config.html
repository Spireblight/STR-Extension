SlayTheRelics Configuration Page

Here is how to setup this extension:
  • Go to your Steam Library on the Slay the Spire page
  • Open the Workshop tab
  • Find and install these mods:
    • ModTheSpire
    • BaseMod
    • SlayTheRelics Exporter
  • Look at the form below this list and click Generate, to create your secret key
  • Click Save to have your secret saved in this extension associated with your channel
  • Open the Slay the Spire folder on your disk (PathToYourSteamLibrary/steamapps/common/SlayTheSpire)
    • Sreate a file in this folder named slaytherelics_config.txt (so that it's in the same folder as the SlayTheSpire.exe)
    • Write your login and copy the generated secret in the following format (replace <your_login> and <your_generated_secret> with your credentials):
      login:<your_login>
      secret:<your_generated_secret>
    • Save the file
  • Open your Steam Library, select Slay the Spire and click play
  • A popup will ask whether to play with mods, select to play with mods
  • A mod launcher window appears, make sure that you have BaseMod and SlayTheRelics Exporter mods selected and hit Play
  • You're good to go! Your game will now be able to send data to this extension.
